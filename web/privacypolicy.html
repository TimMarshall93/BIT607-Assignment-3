Privacy Statement


In accordance with the Privacy Act 2022, we collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  1. name
  2. contact information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  1. provide a service to our customers and to contact them regarding their reservations

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter contact details, we'll be unable to contact them regarding their reservation.

We keep your information safe by storing it password protected on a secure server and only allowing appropriate staff members to access it.

We keep your information for one year at which point we destroy it by securely erasing all collected data.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at haukairestaurant@gmail.com, or 027 123 4567, or 12 Waipuku Rd, Bay of Islands.
